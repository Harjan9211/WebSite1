PGDCA

PGDCA means Post Graduate Diploma In Computer Applications. It is a course in Auckland for graduate students interested in Computer applications. This course was started for people to learn computer applications in different fields like banking, insurance and accounting. PGDCA allows students to seek professional computer knowledge
