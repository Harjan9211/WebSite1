B.C.A. or Bachelor of Computer Application is a course which is nearly equivalent with B.Tech/B.E in Computer Science or Information technology.Bachelor of Computer Application is a 3-year undergraduate programme. It is extended over six semesters. The course is designed to bridge the gap between IT industries and institutes by incorporating the latest developments into the curriculum.which helps the students to update themselves with the recent trends in the industry.The vision of the Course and its goal to “develop IT professionals with ethical values”.It focuses on empowering students with competencies in theoretical, programming, communication, team work, organisation and management skills

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a bachelor's degree in Commerce and business administration. In most universities, the degree is conferred upon a student after four years of full-time study (120 credit hours) in one or more areas of business concentrations; see below. The BBA program usually includes general business courses and advanced courses for specific concentrations
